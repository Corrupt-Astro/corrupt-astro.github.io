About Origin

Origin is a developer focused on exploring the boundaries of web creation, from elegant, production-ready applications to, well, *less* conventional projects. I believe understanding bad design is just as crucial as mastering good design. This is a portfolio dedicated to both the triumphs and the hilarious failures that happen along the way.
